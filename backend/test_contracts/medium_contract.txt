Freelance Services Agreement

Between: Tech Solutions Co. ("Client") and the Freelancer.

1. Scope of Work
Freelancer will design and develop a mobile application as discussed in meetings.
Additional features may be requested by the client at their discretion.

2. Payment
Total project fee: 8,000 SAR.
25% upfront upon signing, remaining 75% upon final delivery and client approval.
No late payment penalties specified.

3. Timeline
Project to be completed within 10 weeks of signing.
Client may request timeline changes with 3 days notice.

4. Revisions
Up to 3 rounds of revisions are included in the project fee.
Additional revisions will be charged at a rate to be agreed upon separately.

5. Intellectual Property
All work product transfers to the client upon receipt of full payment.
Freelancer may not display the work in their portfolio without written permission.

6. Confidentiality
Freelancer agrees to keep all client information confidential during the contract period.
Confidentiality expires 1 year after contract end.

7. Termination
Either party may terminate with 14 days written notice.
Client will pay for work completed up to the termination date.

8. Disputes
Any disputes will be resolved under Saudi Arabian law.
No specific court or arbitration body is named.
